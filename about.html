📖 My Story

I'm an aspiring AI/ML engineer from India, passionate about building impactful AI solutions through research, community learning, and hands-on experimentation.

Over the course of my studies, I've developed a strong foundation in software development, data structures, and emerging technologies. I've worked on end-to-end projects that helped me grow not just as a developer, but also as a learner and team player.

I believe in the power of continuous learning and sharing knowledge. That's why I actively contribute to open source projects and write technical blogs to help others on their journey. I'm a strong believer in #BuildInPublic – learning in public and sharing both successes and failures.

As I step into the professional world, I aim to contribute to impactful projects, sharpen my expertise, and evolve into a skilled engineer and researcher who bridges the gap between ideas and practical applications.
